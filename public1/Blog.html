Ancient Ajanta Caves

The Ajanta Caves, located in Maharashtra, are renowned for their exquisite ancient Buddhist art and architecture. Carved into rugged cliffs between the 2nd century BCE and 6th century CE, these caves feature intricate murals and sculptures depicting Jataka tales and Buddhist teachings. A UNESCO World Heritage site, the Ajanta Caves remain a testament to early Indian artistry and religious devotion.
